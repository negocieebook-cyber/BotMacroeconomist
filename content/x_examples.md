## Approved Style References

### Valor
- Oil chart post: opens with the one chart that matters, then explains the setup and the implication.
- Kobeissi reaction: takes a noisy headline, strips it down to the relevant number, and reframes the narrative.

### Crescimento
- "Don't be short-sighted": sharp hook, clear trend, wider implication for positioning and attention.

### Personalidade
- "Most people lose by reacting": process-driven, calm, based on discipline rather than prediction.

### Prova
- Leo Ruga DCA quote: thesis validated by behavior and outcomes, not by abstract opinion.

## Style Notes
- English only.
- No emojis.
- Short sentences.
- Use `-->` when a list improves readability.
- Prefer one strong hook over multiple ideas.
